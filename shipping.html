Surprivo products are offered on a pre‑order / presale basis. Orders are usually dispatched within 16–30 days after the presale ends and production is completed. Delivery time after dispatch depends on your location and the courier partner. ​ Shipping is currently available within India only. Any shipping charges are shown before checkout or clearly mentioned in the order details. You will receive a dispatch update and, where available, a tracking link by email or message. Delivery delays can occur due to courier issues, strikes, weather or other events outside Surprivo’s control. If your parcel is significantly delayed after dispatch, please contact us using the details on the Contact Us page.
